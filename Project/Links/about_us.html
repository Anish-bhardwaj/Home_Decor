Home Decor is a goods and service based company.Which enables you to purchase or hire Goods and services according to your use. It is established by a group of students in 2022 and providing its services and goods to all over jaipur.At Home Decor , we document inspiration. Our aim is to bring to our customers a steady set of resources that would help them visualize, create and maintain beautiful homes. Our emphasis is on visuals that motivate you. We cover architectural innovations, cool homes, ideas for specific rooms, new design trends, products and occasionally decor tips. We hope to become your one stop source for home design inspiration!

We are a close knit team of architects, interior designer – decorators and civil engineers who are able to translate the client’s ideas into successful projects which work both aesthetically and commercially. Our designs and executions have integrity and our clients reap the rewards. We work from “concept to completion”.

Home Decor is an attempt to change the game. We would like to bring more recognition and visibility to architects and interior designers who build beautiful places for us to live and work in! We hope that this in turn would bring about a change in the way their potential clients perceive them and help them win more of both - business and fame.

We hope to provide the design community with a high-tech platform to build their reputation, transact business as per the trends emerging worldwide and have access to never before offers from manufacturers and vendors. We excel in providing the interiors to client's taste, be it a ultra modern minimalistic look, or traditional ethnic look or rustic country style interiors or the soft feminine look or sometimes monochromatic single colour interior and develop the designs as the clients want their interiors to be done.

Our creative team ensures you get excellent, customized design solutions, whereas our quality management systems assure you of timely completion of your project & all this in a budget that suits you. We believe in listening to our client's ideas, dreams and aspirations and then using our technical knowhow, design expertise and experience we bring those dreams to reality. From a home owner perspective, we are here to change the way they find architects, interior designers, contractors or even design ideas. Whatever may your budget be - Rs. 500 or Rs. 5,00,000 , we hope that you will find ideas as well as professionals here, to build your dream space in that budget.
